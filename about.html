Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Click on the "Sign Up" button in the top-right corner of the website, fill out the registration form, and verify your email address.

How can I apply for a job?

Once you've created an account, you can browse through job listings, filter based on your preferences, and click the "Apply Now" button on the job listing you're interested in.

How do I post a job listing?

If you're an employer, you can click on the "Post a Job" link in the navigation menu, fill out the job listing form, and submit it for review.
